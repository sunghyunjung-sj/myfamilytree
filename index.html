ℹ️ How to Use Family Tree

1. Multi-Select & Move (Crucial) 🟦

Hold the [Shift] key + Drag your mouse on the background to draw a blue box. You can select multiple people and move them together.

2. Smart Linking 🔗

Click a link button (e.g., 🔗 Child), then click the Parent, and finally click the Child.
*Tip: You only need to link ONE parent to a child. The system automatically finds the spouse and draws a neat line from the center!

3. Align & Organize 📏

Select multiple people using Shift+Drag. Then, use the [↕️ Align] button to match their heights, and the [↔️ Space] button to spread them equally.

4. Edit Profile & Photo 📸

Double-click any person's card to edit their Name, Birth Year, and upload a Photo.

5. Custom Print 🖨️

Click [🖨️ Print Area], drag a box over the family members you want to capture, and click 'Print!'. It will perfectly fit on an A4 paper.
