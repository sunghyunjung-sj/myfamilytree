ℹ️ How to Use Family Tree

1. Multi-Select & Move 🟦

PC: Hold [Shift] + Drag on the background to draw a blue box.
Mobile: Touch and drag cards individually.

2. Auto-Layout ✨

Click [✨ Auto-Layout] to automatically align ONLY the heights (Y-axis) based on generations. Your horizontal spacing (X-axis) will stay perfectly intact!

3. Smart Linking 🔗

Click a link button (e.g., 🔗 Child), then click Parent, and finally Child. Only link ONE parent.

4. Image Capture 📸

Click [📸 Capture PNG], freely drag to select the exact area you want, and save it as a high-quality picture.
