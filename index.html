ℹ️ How to Use Family Tree

1. Multi-Select & Move 🟦

PC: Hold [Shift] + Drag on the background to draw a blue box.
Mobile: Touch and drag cards individually.

2. Smart Linking 🔗

Click a link button (e.g., 🔗 Child), then click the Parent, and finally the Child. Only link ONE parent.

3. Print / Save PDF 🖨️

Click [🖨️ Print], freely drag to select the exact area you want. The system will auto-zoom to fit the page perfectly.

4. Undo & Cancel Actions ↩️

Made a mistake? Click [↩️ Undo] or press Ctrl+Z.
Press [ESC] or use your phone's Back Button to cancel any active tool.
